Roadmap of Dreams

1. Our Goal

We are not only shooting for the moon with Ape Invaders, we are shooting for the stars. We believe in this project so much that we will allocate funds from mint as well as royalties to launch a community fund that will be used to grow our community even further.

1 /1

2. The Art

Our award winning artist and designer CJ has worked on such brands as L'Oral, Vodafone, and Monster Energy Drinks along with being a classical portrait painter.

3. Community

Ape Invaders is a community-first project, here to benefit the holders and the diamond hands first. Every decision that we make will be discussed and voted on by the community, and we will never do anything or make any decisions without the overwhelming support of our community.

4. Starting Off

We are starting with a closed Discord to connect with our community on a personal level. Our aim is to launch by January 2022. To stay up to date with everything Ape Invaders please follow us on Twitter at @TheApeInvaders.

5. Whitelist, Discord Access, &Launch

We want to give back to our early supporters. Were doing this by allocating whitelist spots to every single person in our closed Discord community.

You will receive an @OG Invader role to signify that. OGs will be the first to hear about upcoming news, mint dates, and other future promotions.

6. Community Wallet

A community wallet will be set up with 90% of all funds allocated from mint and secondary revenue. These funds will be constantly reinvested into the project and the community to provide further value to our holders.

The community wallet will always be public for all to see so the community can easily follow how the funds are being invested as transparently as possible.

7. Community Giveaways

With our community being our first priority always. We plan on hosting giveaways that will reward random holders for participating. Prizes will vary but you can expect to get free airdrops with extra $Stardust, Ape Invaders, IRL merch and even our extremely limited 1/1s.

8. Stardust

Stardust will be an ERC20 Token. You will receive Stardust by participating in Ape Invasions, holding your Invaders, and through various community rewards. Stardust will be used in our ecosystem to participate in invasions, power up your Apes, and to mint future collections.

1 $Stardust = 1 $Stardust

9. Ape Invasions

All Ape Invaders will be able to participate in Planet Invasions; a planet will be considered conquered once all of its resources (land) has been claimed. To be considered for this planetary conquest, your Ape Invader must be staked in order to participate.

Ape Invader holders will passively earn $Stardust, but actively participating in the war effort will earn you a greater share of $Stardust. The amount of land that is acquired after a planet is conquered, determines the amount of $Stardust you are rewarded. Unique 1/1s will be released that will have a land earning bonus compared to regular apes.

10. Rewards

Every Ape Invader Holder will be rewarded. These rewards will vary but you can expect to earn $Stardust, Exclusive Airdrops, and Physical Merch.

11. NFT Banners

After a planet is conquered Ape Invaders that lead the conquest will be able to generate custom banners to show off their NFTs in exclusive social profile style banners. These will be created with the conquered planets theme.

12. Ranks

Each Ape Invader will gain ranks from participating in conquests. These ranks will increase the amount of land gained and open up exclusive rank-specific perks.

Top Ranks will receive badges that can be used to display in their PFP they will also receive physical plaques &medals.

13. Future Plans

Future Planets After our initial planet is conquered, there will be new planets to claim with unique mechanics.

Second Collection A mysterious second collection awaits!

Ape Invasion Factions In the future greedy Apes have decided to band together and claim all the $Stardust for themselves!

Invasion Investments Prior to each war starting Invaders will be able to invest into their chosen faction. Like any investment, these will come with some risk!

Seasonal Bonuses All future planets will be unique, some will be themed around real world events and holidays.

Alpha Passes Our Alpha Pass will offer guaranteed game rewards, project collab whitelists, and additional value for holders.

Token Burns We will have various $Stardust burns along the way to ensure that theres never too much Stardust floating around.

and so will Ape Invaders plans and goals!

FAQ'S

When are the Ape Invaders launching?

Currently we are aiming for a Mid January mint. Our team is working hard to ensure that everything will be ready and live at launch. We want to get you earning $Stardust right away!

What blockchain will Ape Invaders be on?

We will be on the Ethereum Blockchain, and our $Stardust Token will be ERC-20.

What is the minting price?

0.055 ETH

How many can be minted per wallet?

Up to 5 Ape Invaders per wallet.

When is the game going to be available?

The game will be playable after metadata is revealed, shortly after public minting.

Is there any utility?

Yes! Loads of it infact, starting off each Ape Invader will be able to invade planets in return for rewards. This will be initated by Staking. Each Ape Invader that is staked on a planet will return land. The more land you conquer (by staking longer), the more $Stardust you are rewarded.

Not only that, specific planets will also have bonus rewards which can include but are not limited to Airdrops, Physical Merch, Ape Invaders, and more!

Will $Stardust have any value?

$Stardust will be the currency of Ape Invaders. Staking your Ape Invader will earn an amount of $Stardust per day, you will also earn $Stardust based on percentage of land claimed during Ape Invasions.

1 $Stardust = 1 $Stardust, although a community memeber is free to setup a liquidity pool if they choose to.
